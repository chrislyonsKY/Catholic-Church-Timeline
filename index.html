    The first witnesses

    Apostles in focus

    The Twelve form the foundation of the apostolic college. Matthias replaces Judas Iscariot; Paul and Barnabas carry the mission outward; Mary Magdalene is honored as the witness sent to announce the Resurrection.

    Holiness through history

    Saints across the centuries

    Not a separate line from Church history, but people within it: martyrs, bishops, monastics, scholars, missionaries, reformers, and servants of the poor. Filter the gallery to follow changing forms of Christian witness.

    The dates describe earthly life; “c.” indicates approximation.

    This is a representative reading path, not a ranking or a complete calendar. The worldwide Catholic Church venerates saints from far more communities, cultures, vocations, and periods than a single timeline can display.
